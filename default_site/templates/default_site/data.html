Databases

Why do you NEED one?

Here's a few reasons...

Firstly data doesn't mean saving people ages and birthdays to sell on to cold callers... that's not our goal, here at MakeWebWork the data we talk about is things that will help you help your customers

For example an online store might benefit from knowing what is in a customers wishlist or basket. If they signed in during their visit they would have provided an email and let us know their marketing preferance. Let's say you saw a product that's in 70 of you potential customers basket. So you change the price normally that wouldn't mean anything but for those that have selected yes to marketing emails will receive and email saying the price has changed with a direct link to the website. When they return we would have saved their basket meaning that they will also have everything else they had selected. Data is more powerful than you realise.

Wait, did you say sign in?

Yeah another thing databases enable you to support where else can you store your customers passwords and usernames safely? There are so many ways databases will support your business. Data allows you to create a genuinely personalised experience for you customers. Got a great customer you appreciate let them know with a sign in message! The possibilities are endless when it comes to data. It really is up to you how you want to use it, let us worry about implementing the functionality.

Data helps you understand your audience

Order history, financial information, product performance, website performance, most visited pages, time spent on each page. These are all things that could help you understand your customers. Maybe you want to collect feedback from customers: Why didn't they buy? Why are they leaving the site? Do they plan on coming back? All things made possible by databases

Where's the catch?

Databases need to be managed, indexes need to be setup, tables need to be provisioned, access restrictions need to be set, they need to be monitored for bad data....

Wait, why are we telling YOU this?

We do all that.
